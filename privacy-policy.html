Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 3, 2025

Prompt Scientist ("we", "our", or "us") operates https://prompt-sci.pro. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how we store it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for an account on Prompt Scientist, we collect the following information:

  • Full name

  • Password (securely stored — see Data Security)

We do not require or collect phone numbers, payment details, or physical addresses to use the core platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Create and manage your account and profile.

  • Send essential account and security notifications.

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties for marketing. Any sharing with third parties (such as privacy-focused analytics or authentication providers) will be disclosed and minimized.

Content you publish (prompts, images, descriptions) is public by default and can be viewed and reused by other users in accordance with our Terms of Service. You retain responsibility for ensuring that content you upload or share complies with applicable laws and does not infringe third-party rights.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative safeguards to protect personal data. Passwords are hashed and salted before storage. Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel. Regular security reviews and updates are performed.

Despite these measures, no internet-based system can be guaranteed 100% secure. If a security incident affecting personal data occurs, we will follow applicable laws and notify affected users as required.

5. Cookies & Session Storage

The site may use minimal session cookies to maintain authenticated sessions. We do not employ advertising trackers. You can manage cookies via your browser, but disabling essential cookies may affect functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

Where we use third-party services (for example, for optional analytics or authentication), we aim to select privacy-respecting providers and limit data sharing. Any such integrations will be described in this policy and in the site UI where applicable.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. Note: public content you posted may remain visible unless removed from the platform.

8. Children

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us personal information, contact us and we will promptly remove it.
